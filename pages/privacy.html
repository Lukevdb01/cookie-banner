2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to help the website function and improve your experience. They may be temporary (session cookies) or remain for a longer period (persistent cookies).

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Ensure the website functions correctly
  • Analyze traffic and usage statistics
  • Remember your preferences (e.g., language or theme)
  • Improve performance and usability

4. Types of Cookies We Use

Analytics Understand how users interact with the website
Preferences Store user settings and customization
Third-party (optional) Set by embedded services such as videos or social media widgets

Note that some parts of the site may not function properly if cookies are disabled.
